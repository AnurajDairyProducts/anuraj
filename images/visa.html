      What is visa image?
      The US visa photo size should be 2 x 2 inches (51 x 51 mm). The photo should be positioned in a way that the head is between 1 and 1 3/8 inches (22 mm and 35 mm) or 50% and 69% of the image's total height from the bottom of your chin to the top of the head. In color. Taken within the last 6 months. With no shadows.
